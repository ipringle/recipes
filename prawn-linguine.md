# Prawn Linguine

## Ingredients
- 2 packs prawns
- 1 onion
- 1 x 400g tin chopped tomatos
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 red chilli
- 1 lemon
- 200ml double cream

## Method
- Chop and Fry onions
- Add chopped tomato
- Add salt, pepper, garlic
- Grate in chilli
- Reduce down
- Add cream
- Add prawns
- Zest lemon
- Add al dente pasta to sauce for a few minutes at the end
